Challenges and Opportunities

Venture Businesses are finding that heavy investments in executive level positions are required to speed market introductions. In most venture companies, this requirement may far outweigh budget resources. Considering the financial constraints, how do you attract top executives? In a business of any size, this investment warrants a more comprehensive and time consuming selection process to insure the quality and "fit" of senior level managers. The search could take months, how do you manage your company in the interim? "Outsourcing" executive assigments which have a critical path of limited duration has become a preferred practice. How do you find an executive consultant with the knowledge and experience to help your organization realize its true potential?
